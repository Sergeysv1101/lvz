HIGH-PRECISION UNIVERSAL CIRCULAR TURN-ING MACHINES models 3S120V, 3S130V, 3S132V, 3S133V

SEMI-AUTOMATIC CYLINDRICAL GRINDING MACHINES models 3M173, 3M174, 3M175
Semi-automatic cylindrical grinding machines are designed for external grinding of cylindrical and shallow conical surfaces of parts by the method of plunge and longitudinal grinding in conditions of single, serial and large-scale production. The following are automated and mechanized on the semi-automatic machine: plunge and longitudinal grinding methods, change of transverse, longitudinal and circular feeds; start and stop of the product; switching on and off of the coolant; rapid supply and withdrawal of the grinding head and tailstock quill; grinding head travel along the screw; balancing of the grinding wheel. By agreement with the customer, semi-automatic machines can be produced adjusted for grinding a specific product. Semi-automatic machines have semi-automatic control and have the possibility of manual control. The semi-automatic cycle is provided by a mechanical device with electro-hydraulic control. The semi-automatic machine model 3M175 can be manufactured with a setting for grinding the surfaces of the neck and the step part of the railway axle RU1Sh in one workpiece installation, both during the manufacture and repair of wheelsets.

MACHINES FOR GRINDING CRANKSHAFTS models 3411, 3D4230, 3V423, 3D4231, LT-235
The machines are designed for regrinding of main and connecting rod journals of crankshafts of automobile, tractor and diesel engines, compressors and other crank-type parts in conditions of auto repair, tractor plants, workshops and other repair services engaged in restoration of crankshafts. CNC MACHINING CENTER "Sinumerik 840D" model MC665-2-Sin840
The MC665-2-Sin840 machining center is a five-axis machine tool for machining body parts at high cutting speeds during milling. The machine has a common T-shaped bed on which the oscillation guides with rollers are installed. The following move along the guides: the carriage (power rotary table X-axis); longitudinal-transverse rack (Z-axis). The movable headstock (Y-axis) moves along the rack (along the oscillation guides). The drives of the longitudinal, transverse and vertical movements are carried out from high-torque servo motors through ball screw gears. Oversized and very precise guides with rollers and proportional ball screw gears ensure reliability and accuracy of milling. The power rotary tables (A-axis and B-axis) are driven directly from toroidal electric motors. The horizontal table B acts as a carriage and is mounted directly on the carriages of the oscillation guides. A vertical table A is mounted on the table through a spacer. During operation, the tables are hydraulically opened. When the torus motor is not working or in the event of a voltage drop in the network, the tables are clamped by disc brakes. To prevent the working parts of the machine from any unauthorized movements (during power outages or emergency stops), all feed drive motors are equipped with braking devices. The cutting tool is fixed in the spindle head of the milling machine.
